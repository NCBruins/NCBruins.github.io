Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you offer?

We offer divisions for U10 (ages 8-10), U12 (ages 11-12), U14 (ages 13-14), and U16 (ages 15-16).

When is the season?

Season dates are TBD. Please check our schedule page or contact us for the most current information.

How do I register my child?

Visit our Registration page for complete details on how to sign up.

What experience level is required?

We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced players. Our coaches work with each player to develop their skills appropriately.

What equipment does my child need?

Players need basketball shoes and athletic clothing for practices and games. Jerseys will be provided as part of registration.

Where are practices and games held?

Locations are TBD. All practice and game locations will be communicated to registered families.

Can parents volunteer?

Absolutely! We welcome parent volunteers for various roles including coaching assistants, team managers, and event helpers. Please contact us if you're interested in volunteering.
