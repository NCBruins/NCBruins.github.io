About North City Bruins

The North City Bruins Basketball League is dedicated to providing youth in Toronto with a positive, competitive basketball experience. We believe in developing not just skilled players, but well-rounded individuals who understand the values of teamwork, dedication, and sportsmanship.

Our program focuses on:

  • Fundamental skill development
  • Competitive game experience
  • Character building and leadership
  • Creating a supportive community
