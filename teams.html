About North City Bruins

The North City Bruins Basketball League is dedicated to providing youth in Toronto with a positive, competitive basketball experience. We believe in developing not just skilled players, but well-rounded individuals who understand the values of teamwork, dedication, and sportsmanship.

Our Mission

To provide a positive environment where young athletes can develop their basketball skills and character.

Age Divisions

U10, U12, U14, and U16 divisions designed to match skill levels and ensure competitive, fun play.

Professional Coaching

Experienced coaches dedicated to player development and creating a positive learning environment.
